The Dive

About

Centrally located in downtown Ottawa, The Dive offers aquatic programs and specialized programs for individuals with special needs. The Dive has 25m & 50m pools, as well as a child's pool and swirl pool. The Dive also offers public swimming and fitness programs for all. Make a splash and have some fun jumping off our diving boards!
